My name is Maya Jain. I am a multi-disciplinary designer. I strive to prioritize care in a world governed by profit. Through design, I hope to empower in ways that grant individuals, and communities the freedom to thrive and cultivate joy . I’d like to think that my work exists at the intersection of an active, politically engaged practice and an artistic, philosophical, creative one. My background is in a variety of media that range from agriculture and food to fashion design.
